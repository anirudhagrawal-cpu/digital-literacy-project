Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
# Do’s
1) Use social media in a responsible and professional manner. Always remember that your online presence can be seen by teachers, recruiters, and other professionals.
2) Think carefully before posting anything online. Make sure that the content you share is appropriate and does not harm your reputation or others.
3) Protect your personal information such as phone number, address, passwords, and academic details. Keeping your accounts private and secure is very important.
4) Use social media for learning and self-improvement. Follow educational pages, technical communities, and platforms that help you develop new skills.
5) Show respect towards others while communicating online. Always use polite language and avoid posting anything offensive or disrespectful.
# Don’ts
1) Do not share sensitive information such as OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone on social media platforms.
2) Do not post negative, abusive, or offensive comments. Such behaviour can create a bad impression and may also lead to serious consequences.
3) Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information. Always check the authenticity of the content before posting or forwarding it.
4) Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown people without verifying their identity.
5) Avoid spending too much time on social media as it can affect your studies and productivity.
